Instructions:

  1. Once page has loaded click 'Play'.
  2. A randomly selected audio file will play.
    The second note will be sharper of flatter than the first by 30cent.
  3. Using the 'Flat' or 'Sharp' buttons, select whether
    the second pitch is flatter or sharper than the first
  4. If your answer is correct the score will increase
  5. A new audio file will immediately sound. Answer again.
  6. Correct answers build the score - maximum 100. An incorrect answer is Game Over
  7. The cent level decreases as the score increases, shown on the score board.
    A 'cent' is 1/100 of a semitone.
    The cent level decreases by 1:
    30-21cent - with 2 correct
    20-11cent - with 5 correct
    10-1cent - with 3 correct
    The cent level stays at 1 from a score of 97 onwards
